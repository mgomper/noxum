Noxum Diabetes Predictor

The Noxum Diabates Predictor is a proof of concept commisioned by Samures to find out if diabetes can be prevented by predicting diabetes at an early stage using machine learning.

Result: Diabetes Type II

Your results have shown that you are likely to develop type II diabetes. We came to this result by comparing your body values to those of diabetes patients via machine learning.

This advice is not medically binding. However, concidering this result, it might be a good idea to visit your general practitionar to discuss the topic further. Diabetes is partly preventable, we advice you to make certain lifestyle changes. When a person has developed diabetes, it can not be cured.
